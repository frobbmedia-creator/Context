# Terms Template

This is a launch-readiness template, not legal advice.

## Product

Context is a local-first browser utility for preparing workspace files for LLM prompts and JSON payloads.

## Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for:

- Selecting which local files to process.
- Reviewing generated context bundles before submitting them to any LLM or third-party service.
- Complying with licenses and confidentiality obligations for source files they process.

## Product Boundaries

The app performs deterministic local processing. Token counts are local estimates unless a separately bundled exact tokenizer is added.

## Privacy

The app is designed to operate without uploads, telemetry, analytics, or account services.
